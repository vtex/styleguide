
### Added

### Changed

- Dependencies update
- **Modal** prop `content` renamed to `children`

- **Dropdown** and **Input** prop `block`. They are always block.
- **Dropdown** and **Input** props `short` and `long`. Their widths should be defined by their parents.
